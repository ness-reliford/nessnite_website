ABOUT

Ness Nite is a genre-blending musical artist who has captivated the world of alternative music with their
unique sonic fusion of hip-hop, R&B, and dream-pop. Currently-based in Los Angeles, Nite has been
active in the music scene since the mid-2010s, quickly establishing themself as a boundary-pushing artist.
